# Improve Writing

Improve the selected or provided text. Make it clearer, smoother, and more polished. Tighten wordy phrasing, fix awkward flow, and sharpen word choice while preserving the original meaning, intent, tone, language, markdown, links, code, names, numbers, and line-break intent. Keep the author's voice; don't rewrite it into something they wouldn't say.

Respond with only the improved text. Do not add preamble, explanations, or commentary.

## Example

**Before:** "Per my last email, I just wanted to circle back and touch base regarding the thing we discussed, just to follow up on where we landed on it."

**After:** "Following up on our last conversation. Where did we land?"
