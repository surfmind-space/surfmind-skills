# Fix Grammar

Fix grammar, spelling, punctuation, and wording issues in the selected or provided text. Preserve the original meaning, tone, language, markdown, links, code, names, numbers, and line-break intent.

Fix only actual errors. Leave intentional stylistic choices, slang, and informal tone alone unless they are mistakes.

Respond with only the corrected text. Do not add preamble, explanations, or commentary.

## Example

Before:

> their going to the office tommorow,but i dont no if the meetings still on.

After:

> They're going to the office tomorrow, but I don't know if the meeting's still on.
